    The Hallway

    The hallway is lined with old portraits whose eyes seem to follow you. There’s an old cabinet here. A door creaks open ahead of you. Do you enter, or search the cabinet?

    The grand staircase stretches into darkness above. You hear a faint, mournful cry from the upper rooms. Do you ascend the stairs?
